Proficiency & Engagement

Your Big Board Patient Tracking solution uses your new iPad to create a check-in process that involves minimal direct contact. Any time a patient's status changes, Big Board will send it to your Engage communication solution - immediately triggering a text to the patient's ride home. The result? Family members and staff are informed with minimal interaction- and your waiting room remains empty.

Display Information throughout your facility

Big Board takes your scheduling information and displays a HIPAA-compliant version on monitors throughout your facility: a big screen in the waiting room, smaller monitors in pre-op, tablets in each OR— whatever works best for you.

Light & Dark Scheme Available

Engage allows you to customize messages by surgery type, surgeon and even patient health status. Automated reminders and educational videos get sent to patients from pre-op to recovery. Another key feature- you can send specific messages to the patient's family, your staff, and even vendors.
